We host a variety of workshops that include introductory- advanced instruction, Make & Take, Bring Your Own Piece, Cabinet Doors, hands-on teaching workshops, and a wide array of DIY products.

Introduction to Jolie Workshop

In this recurring workshop taught by the owner, Kelly Brannon, you will learn the basics of how to use and maximize Jolie Products. Featuring six techniques to create our most common finishes for furniture, floors, cabinets, accessories, and more.

- Basic

- Smooth

- Textured

- Distressed

- Dry Brushed

- Washed

It’s simple, Jolie is helping you make old things new again!

Kitchen and Bath Cabinet Workshop

Training on how to paint or stain your own kitchen cabinets with instruction from the owner, Kelly Brannon. This workshop simplifies the daunting process of cabinet restoration through detailed step by step instructions. Equipping you with the skills you need to tackle your next project, filling you with a sense of accomplishment. We can also restore your cabinets for you through our custom restoration.
