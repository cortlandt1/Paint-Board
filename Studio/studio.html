Paint in place

The Studio offers a space to explore your creative side, play with color, explore finishes and textures.

Check out our Calendar for workshop and artist sessions and Private Parties.

Paint in Place hours available. $15 hour you may use the studio, receive hands on instruction to get you started request paint color and topcoat consultation, use studio brushes and base supplies. All paint, top coat and embellishment products sold separately.

Private Parties

Studio available for rent for private parties. Starting in the summer, we will be accommodating private parties. We love hosting special events just for you, and welcome bookings. Twelve people maximum. Contact us to plan a unique experience and for available dates and times. Here are a few party ideas we really enjoy:

  • Bridal parties - creating a one-of-a-kind-gifts for the bride
  • Graduation parties - send your senior off to college with the memories of making their own dorm decorations
  • Baby shower gift parties – create decorations for your new baby’s nursery
  • Birthday parties for all ages – send your guest home with memories and mementos
  • Any of our general workshops for your own private party!
