Our Story

Kelly Brannon, owner welcomes you to Paint & Board Boutique and Studio.

Paint & Boards Boutique celebrates creativity and style combining a passion for collaborative design, woodworking, furniture paint and restoration, seasonal gifts and home décor. Retailing Jolie Home Matte Paint Products, Dixie Belle Chalk Paint, Prima Redesign, Mudd Pie & Blue Ocean Trader home goods. Paint & Boards Boutique also ​offers a beautiful range of hand painted, refinished furniture, vintage finds, and repurposed treasures.

PBB Studio offers a place to express your creativity with the company of other customers, family, and friends through a variety of workshops, paint in place studio hours, variety of classes and private parties.

The Studio is also available for birthdays, showers or special events. Please contact Kelly Brannon for pricing and availability.
